About

Hi, I am Xinjie Fan (樊鑫杰 in Chinese), a fourth-year Ph.D. student from the Department of Statistics and Data Sciences at the University of Texas at Austin. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Mingyuan Zhou. My research interest focuses on deep learning and Bayesian statistics. More specifically, I work on reinforcement learning, deep generative models, Bayesian neural network, attention models with applications in image captioning, neural program synthesis, visual question answering, image classification, and etc. I also worked on theoretically guaranteed hyperparameter tuning procedure for clustering problems. Before I came to UT Austin, I obtained my bachelor degree in Mathematics from Beihang University in 2016 and obtained my master degree in Mathematics from Texas A&M University in 2017.
